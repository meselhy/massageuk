Sport massage

The main purpose of sport massage therapy is to help alleviate the stress and tension, which builds up in the body's soft tissues during physical activity. Due to its deep tissue application it is also ideal to treat non sporting injuries. The techniques used often involve a blend of traditional firm muscles and stretching massage specifically designed to treat professional athletes. Sports massage has many benefits. In addition to treating injuries, sport massage also improves the heart rate and blood pressure, improves flexibil-ity. Relive pain and reduces muscle tension.

Shiatsu massage

Shiatsu is traditional. Japanese healing method which very firm pressure techniques and stretching are the keys. The shiatsu practitioner uses palm, fingers, thumbs, knuckles, elbows, knees and feet to apply the pressure on the body's acupuncture points and energy channels. The combination of pressure and stretching deeply relaxes the muscles. Also the shiatsu massage releases toxins, improves flexibility and reduces blood pressure and stress.
